Amara Soul is a Nigerian gospel artist and worship leader whose music blends contemporary Afro-Gospel sounds with deep, Spirit-filled lyricism. Born and raised in Lagos, she discovered her gift for music at age 12 in her local church choir. Her debut single "Fallen Stars" broke records across gospel streaming platforms in 2024, accumulating over 2 million plays within the first month. Known for her powerful soprano voice and anointed delivery, Amara has ministered at major gospel concerts across Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and the UK. Her mission: to use music as a tool of healing, revival, and global evangelism.
